Nordic Sitecore Usergroup Conference

The must-attend event of the year for Sitecore developers and digital marketeers. Stay on top of the development and understand how, when and why. This is the Sitecore event, you don't want to miss

For the first time ever in the Nordic countries, you'll be able to attend a first class Sitecore Usergroup Conference packed with the latest insights and advice from some of the most experienced, skillfull Sitecore experts in the world. You'll get invaluable insights on how to get the most out of the Sitecore Experience platform.

If your are a Sitecore Developer, you can:

  • Learn about all the latest Sitecore products and features in a no sales-bullshit manner
  • Learn about Sitecore best practices using the Sitecore platform by the best people out there
  • Learn some of the secrets and tips the best Sitecore developers use to create outstanding solutions
  • Connect with Sitecore specialists and build a strong network
  • Network with your peers and share Sitecore knowledge

If you are a Digital Marketeer, you can

  • Learn about all the latest Sitecore products and features from a marketing value perspective
  • Learn how to pick your low-hanging Sitecore marketing fruits, so you can start unfolding all those great customer experiences
  • Learn how to use personalisation and engagement plans to increase your conversion
  • Learn how to integrate an omnichannel perspective with the Sitecore platform
